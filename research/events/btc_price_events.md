# BTC Price Target Events

## Event Type
Binary prediction markets on Bitcoin reaching a specific price target by a given date.
Examples: "Will BTC exceed $100K by June 2026?", "Will BTC drop below $50K by Q4 2026?"

## Analysis Framework

### Key Factors
1. **Current price trajectory** — distance from target, momentum direction
2. **Macro environment** — Fed policy, dollar strength, risk-on/risk-off sentiment
3. **On-chain metrics** — exchange reserves, whale accumulation, miner behavior
4. **Derivatives data** — funding rates, open interest, liquidation levels
5. **Halving cycle** — position in the ~4-year cycle (historically bullish 12-18 months post-halving)
6. **ETF flows** — net inflows/outflows to spot BTC ETFs

### Historical Base Rates
- BTC has exceeded previous ATH within 18 months of halving in 3/3 cycles
- BTC drawdowns of 30%+ occur ~2-3 times per bull market cycle
- Round number targets ($100K, $150K) tend to act as psychological resistance

### Common Mispricing Patterns
- Markets often underprice momentum during strong trends
- Markets overprice tail events (extreme crash scenarios)
- Time-based markets (by date X) are sensitive to the distance-to-target vs time-remaining ratio

### Risk Considerations
- High correlation between all BTC price events — watch underlier_group limits
- Volatility spikes can move probabilities 10-20% in hours
- Regulatory news can cause sharp discontinuous moves
