# ETH Price Target Events

## Event Type
Binary prediction markets on Ethereum reaching a specific price target by a given date.

## Analysis Framework

### Key Factors
1. **ETH/BTC ratio** — ETH tends to outperform BTC in mid-to-late bull cycles
2. **Staking metrics** — staking yield, validator queue, total ETH staked
3. **L2 adoption** — activity on Arbitrum, Optimism, Base as proxy for ETH demand
4. **Gas fees / burn rate** — higher fees = more ETH burned = deflationary pressure
5. **DeFi TVL** — total value locked in Ethereum DeFi protocols
6. **ETH ETF status** — spot ETH ETF approval/flows impact

### Historical Base Rates
- ETH has historically lagged BTC in early bull market then outperformed 2-3x
- ETH/BTC ratio tends to bottom 6-12 months before ETH outperformance
- Post-merge deflationary dynamics add new price floor mechanics

### Risk Considerations
- ETH is more volatile than BTC — wider confidence intervals needed
- Regulatory risk around staking (SEC classification concerns)
- Competition from Solana and other L1s could limit upside
