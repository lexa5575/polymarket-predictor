# Crypto ETF Approval Events

## Event Type
Binary markets on whether specific crypto ETFs will be approved by regulators.

## Analysis Framework

### Key Factors
1. **SEC/regulatory signals** — public statements, comment periods, deadlines
2. **Political environment** — pro-crypto vs anti-crypto administration
3. **Precedent** — BTC spot ETF approval (Jan 2024) set a precedent
4. **Issuer activity** — number of applications, amendments, meetings with SEC
5. **Court rulings** — relevant judicial decisions affecting regulatory stance

### Historical Base Rates
- BTC spot ETF: rejected multiple times (2013-2023), approved Jan 2024
- ETH spot ETF: approved mid-2024 following BTC precedent
- Alt-coin ETFs: mixed success, regulatory uncertainty remains high

### Common Mispricing Patterns
- Markets tend to overreact to rumors of approval/rejection
- Deadline-based markets often mispriced near SEC decision windows
- Political regime changes can shift base rates significantly

### Risk Considerations
- Binary outcome with known deadline — high information asymmetry near resolution
- Insider knowledge risk — probabilities can move sharply before announcements
- Low correlation with price events — separate underlier_group
