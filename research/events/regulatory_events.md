# Regulatory Events

## Event Type
Binary markets on crypto regulatory outcomes — bans, frameworks, enforcement actions.

## Analysis Framework

### Key Factors
1. **Legislative pipeline** — bills in progress, committee hearings
2. **Enforcement actions** — SEC/CFTC cases and their precedent value
3. **International coordination** — G20, FATF guidelines, MiCA (EU)
4. **Industry lobbying** — crypto PAC spending, industry coalition activities
5. **Public sentiment** — voter polling on crypto regulation

### Historical Base Rates
- Major crypto bans: China (2021) was the most significant
- US regulatory frameworks: slow progress, multiple competing bills
- Enforcement-first approach has dominated in the US (2023-2024)

### Risk Considerations
- Regulatory events have wide-ranging market impact
- Long resolution timelines — capital tied up for extended periods
- Political risk: elections and appointees can shift regulatory direction
- Low liquidity on many regulatory markets
